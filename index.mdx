## What Shield detects

- **Hate speech & harassment** — racial slurs, targeted abuse, coordinated attacks
- **Spam** — promotional content, phishing, coordinated inauthentic behavior  
- **NSFW content** — adult content, graphic violence, disturbing imagery
- **Threats** — direct threats, incitement to violence
- **Misinformation** — synthetic media, manipulated content

## Why Shield

Most moderation APIs are keyword filters from 2010. Shield uses:

- **toxic-bert** for text — fine-tuned on real hate speech datasets
- **CLIP** for images and video — understands visual context, not just pixels
- **Whisper** for audio — transcribes and classifies speech in 50+ languages
- **Context engine** — adjusts decisions based on platform type and user history
